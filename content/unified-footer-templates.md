## Usage Guidelines

### symbi.world
- **Purpose:** Community & public onboarding
- **Emphasis:** Non-financial participation, learning, and contribution
- **Footer placement:** After all page content, before any scripts

### gammatria.com
- **Purpose:** Research & governance hub
- **Emphasis:** Constitutional governance, academic credibility, transparency
- **Footer placement:** After main content, can include links to /constitution or /dao above license

### yseeku.com
- **Purpose:** Enterprise revenue & professional services
- **Emphasis:** Production-grade infrastructure, compliance, commercial operations
- **Footer placement:** Concise version that acknowledges the shared ethical base

## Legal Compliance Notes

- **CC BY-NC-ND 4.0** chosen to prevent commercial derivatives while allowing educational use
- **Non-financial language** throughout prevents securities interpretation
- **Transparent audit trail** references support regulatory compliance
- **Australian AI Ethics Principles** alignment supports ARC Discovery applications
